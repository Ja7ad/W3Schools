1. Metaphysics

Metaphysics is the part of Philosophy that studies the nature of Reality.

Meta means "About". Metaphysics means "About Physics" or "About Reality".

What is Rality?

When we look around, we can see a lot of things:

  • Nature
  • Animals
  • People
  • Houses
  • Cars

In Metaphysics, the questions is:

  • What is real?
  • Is what we see real?
  • Is there more than we see?
  • Is there more than we sence?
  • Is there something else?
  • Is there something more?
  • Is there another dimension?
